Big Hero Robot

Big Hero is a robotics academy that offers a wide range of courses for students of different age groups. We guide our students by offering expert advice for their future careers in robotics and related fields. Our hands-on approach allows students to interact with real-world robotics systems, applying what they've learned in the classroom. Additionally, we organize large-scale competitions both in Egypt and internationally, where students can showcase their skills and compete on the global stage.

We offer a range of courses tailored to different age groups and skill levels. For younger students (ages 5 to 9), we provide introductory Lego robotics courses. For students between 9 to 16 years old, we offer electronics and basic robotics courses. For university students, we have advanced courses covering various topics in robotics, electronics, AI, and more. We also offer flexibility in course selection, allowing students to choose based on their needs and interests.
